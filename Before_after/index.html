Aston Martin

Gaydon, Warwickshire, England, United Kingdom

Aston Martin Lagonda Global Holdings plc is a British independent manufacturer of luxury sports cars and grand tourers. It was founded in 1913 by Lionel Martin and Robert Bamford.

Steered from 1947 by David Brown, it became associated with expensive grand touring cars in the 1950s and 1960s, and with the fictional character James Bond following his use of a DB5 model in the 1964 film Goldfinger. Their sports cars are regarded as a British cultural icon.

Olimpiyskiy National Stadium

Kyiv, Ukraine

The stadium is the premier sports venue in Ukraine. It hosted the final of Euro 2012 and the 2018 UEFA Champions League Final.
