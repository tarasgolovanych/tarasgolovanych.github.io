What we do

Highly Efficient

Our triple-pane windows have U-values as low 0.12 or up to 50% more efficient rhan standart American windows.

Actually Affordable

Prices start at $22 per square foot, comparable to standart American windows.

Design-Centered

Structurally reinforced frames, thicker glass and incleased thermal efficiency allows for larger windows, more light, and more expansive views

Passive House Friendly

Our windows are suitable for Passive House, but can be used to make any project more sustainable, comfortable and beautiful.

Completely Customizable

From hardware components, frame finish, handle style and glazing type, we make it easy to get windows that have been custom-built to meet your specifications exactly.

Full Serviceable

Our windows are backed by 15-year warranty against factory defect, and each component is serviceable from a variety of sources in the U.S.

No Matter Your Project, We Have the Tools to Succeed.

Different typologies have different needs. At Mavrik, we have a team of designers and engineers to match a window solution to your project. Many of our systems have commercial ratings achieved through US lab tests. If we don’t have a solution tested to your project’s specs, we can design and test a custom solution to be a perfect fit.

Single family

Expansive views, large sliders and custom modern entry doors are just daily business for us. And there’s just nothing like that European feel. Your home will look amazing inside and out, and your energy bills will be lower to boot!

Multi family passive house

This is our #1 specialty! We can value engineer your windows and install details to give you the best path to Passive House success. When you add the fact that our windows are cost competitive to their US alternatives, it really is a no-brainer!

Affordable housing

No longer doess affordable housing equate with low-performance windows! We have solutions for any size affordable project to help you make it green and save money for the future residents and owners. With so many programs giving preference to efficiency, we can help you get a leg up on the bidding process!

Luxury residential

In a highly competitive market, every detail counts. Blow away your prospective residents with expansive views and high quality European hardware. Add green credentials and noise reduction with triple pane glass: all while saving money!
